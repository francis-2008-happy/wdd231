Website Plan

Lekki Stake Young Single Adults Connect

Site Name

Lekki Stake Young Single Adults Connect

This name clearly identifies the target audience (Young Single Adults ages 18-30) and location (Lekki Stake, Lagos, Nigeria) while emphasizing the site's purpose of connection. It aligns with Church naming conventions while being specific enough to serve its intended audience.

Optional domain availability: lekkistakeysa.org or lekkiysaconnect.ng

Site Purpose

The Lekki Stake Young Single Adults Connect website will serve as an engaging, spiritually uplifting online platform for Young Single Adults (ages 18-30) in Lekki Stake, The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. The site will:

  • Keep YSAs updated on upcoming events (firesides, service projects, dances, institute classes)
  • Provide spiritual resources tailored for YSAs (Come, Follow Me study aids, mission prep materials)
  • Facilitate social connections within the YSA community
  • Offer service opportunities and ways to get involved
  • Create a moderated space for gospel-centered discussions

The website will be faith-promoting, practical, and engagingâ€”helping YSAs in Lekki Stake stay connected to the Church and each other.

Scenarios

These are questions potential site visitors might ask:

  1. "What YSA activities are happening this month in Lekki Stake?" - The site will have an events calendar and announcements section highlighting upcoming activities.
  2. "Where can I find institute classes near me?" - The site will include institute class schedules for Lekki and nearby areas with locations and times.
  3. "How can I prepare for a mission as a YSA in Lagos?" - The site will provide mission prep materials and information about local mission prep classes.
  4. "Is there a way to connect with other YSAs in my stake?" - The site will feature a ward directory, discussion forum, and testimony-sharing section.
